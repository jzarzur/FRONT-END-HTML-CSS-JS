About the 17yo singer:

Billie Eilish is an american singer and song writer. Her first single is called "Ocean eyes" written by her big brother Finneas. Ocean eyes it's part of her first record called "Don't smile at me".

When we all fall asleep where do we go?

The debut album of Billie Eilish was one of the most critically acclaimed albums of the year, considered one of the best albums of 2019 and it's the second album of the 17-year-old singer's career.

Billie Eilish and her brother Finneas produced the entire album at their California home. The album includes the popular tracks "Bad guy" and "Bury a friend", among other tracks that deserve attention for their creativity.
